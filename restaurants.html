This is a page of my favorite restaurants in Portland Oregon

Here are my favorite restaurants in order of how much I like them

  • Voodoo Donuts
  • Big Burger
  • Auntie Annes
  • Happy Family
  • My Kitchen

Many of these places hold valuable memories from my childhood. I grew up in a small town called Molalla outside of Portland. I have fond memores of eating maple bacon donuts and other fancy donuts from Voodoo. Big Burger has the best milkshakes in the city. I miss eating at some of these places that only exist in Portland.

Thank you for visiting my page!
